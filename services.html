Safe Deposit Lock Boxes

Washita Sate Bank can help you protect valuable items such as vehicle titles, contracts, birth certificates, wills, and trusts. These items are securely locked inside the bank vault.  

  • Available in a variety of sizes and annual fees
    • 3 x 5 $20.00 per year
    • 3 x 10 $30.00 per year
    • 5 x 10 $50.00 per year

Night Drop

  • A deposit may be placed in the Night Drop slots located next to the Bank's drive-up. 
  • Simply fill out an envelope and place the sealed envelope in the Night Drop.  The deposit will be collected and processed within 1 business day.

 

Telephone Banking

Interactive Voice Response (IVR) allows customers to obtain account information over the phone 24 hours a day. Please call 1-866-862-8061 to obtain account information.
